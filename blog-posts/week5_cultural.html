Week 5 Cultural:

Pairing

I have a great time pairing with others to accomplish/finish challenges. I feel more productive in many ways. The problems are solved quicker, and I'm not as easily distracated. It also felt great going through a challenge and coding without having to refer to doing too much in the way of searches. Like I'm finally learning or accomplishing something. Pairing removes a lot of the frustrations of learning alone especially when I feel like I've hit a wall.

Feedback

I was able to read through the feedback that I have received yesterday. It seems like I tend to minimize the value that I bring to my peer pairing sessions. All of those little negative self talk voices had me feeling inadequet, but as the sessions progressed I noticed a certain comfort level developing.

It seems that I do need to speak up more when things start going a little off track. I tend to let us go in the wrong direction with the assumption that my pair knows way more than I do and that they have a better method in mind. Receiving feedback will help me better work with peers in the future, and being able to give people that sam opportunity to improve feels great.
