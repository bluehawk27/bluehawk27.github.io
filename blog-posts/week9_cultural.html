Week 9 Cultural

Whats a good Question?

Any question we ask is also asking for a persons personal time. This makes it important to be considerate of other peoples time when we are asking for help or questions on any board. It is important to be prepared with specifics and to give a little background on the issue. Try not to be long winded and get to the point. So how do you specifically ask good questions.

Give specific background on the issue. Be detailed and susinct. Most of the time your initial top layer description should be enough.

Give a summarized description of what you have tried. Try not to be vague or trail off.

Listen to feedback and respond to anyone who responds. Remember that they have taken time out of their schedule to help you.

Mind Your Content!
