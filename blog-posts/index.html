Hello! If you've landed here you are no doubt interested in either my time at DBC or I included a link to this site im my resume.

I have a varied past with experiences that have ranged from something as simple as attending college and USMC Officer Candidate School to drag racing amd skydiving for my 30th birthday party with family and friends.

My work histroy is pretty diverse as well. I've worked at the airport on the ramp fueling private jets at Atlantic Aviation in SJ. I opend a couple of companies as well one serviced those same aircraft on the field. The other company concentrated on building maintenance. I've sold insurance as an agent and currently I just wrapped up my studies at Dev Bootcamp.

So with all of that you may be thinking, "geeez! This guy wont sit still!". While it's true that I have dabbled in a lot, I have walked away from each experience with an appreciation for the nuances that all the positions required. I also learned that all of them share something in common. That being the basic interaction with people and how words/actions can and will affect their perception of you. In that regard most positions and endevours have lead me to the conclusion that honesty, trustworthiness, owning mistakes, and doing what you say you will do, will be characteristics and traits that are respected by all.

Following that simple formula in every endevour has left many doors open and has in fact opened up many more as well. As I pursue my job hunt post DBC I will continue to update this blog on my experiences, success' and inevitable failures.
